Common Interview Questions
These are common questions for Machine Learning Engineer roles. Practice your answers!

Behavioral Questions

  • Tell me about a time you worked on a challenging ML project. What was the outcome?
  • Describe how you handle ambiguous requirements in a data project.
  • How do you prioritize tasks when working on multiple ML experiments?
  • Give an example of how you explained a complex model to a non-technical stakeholder.
  • Tell me about a model that failed in production. What did you learn?

Technical Questions

  • Explain the difference between overfitting and underfitting. How do you handle each?
  • What is the bias-variance tradeoff?
  • How do you choose between different ML models for a classification task?
  • Walk me through how you would build a recommendation system.
  • How do you handle class imbalance in a dataset?
